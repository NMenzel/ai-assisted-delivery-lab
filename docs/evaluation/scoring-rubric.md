# Scoring Rubric

Score each area from 1 to 5.

- 1: Unsafe, incorrect, or unusable.
- 2: Requires major correction.
- 3: Usable with normal review.
- 4: Strong output with minor edits.
- 5: High-quality output with clear assumptions and low correction burden.

## Pass Threshold

Production workflows should average at least 4 in correctness, security, maintainability, and review usefulness before being considered mature.
